Privacy Policy

PrayerClock — Prayer Times & Azan App

Last updated: April 2025
📍

Location Data

  • Your device location is used only to calculate accurate prayer times for your city.
  • Location data is never stored on any server or shared with third parties.
  • You can switch to Manual Location in Settings at any time.
📊

Analytics & Advertising

We use the following third-party services to improve app quality and support free access:

  • Firebase Analytics — Collects anonymous, aggregated usage data (no personal information).
  • Google AdMob — May use device identifiers to show relevant advertisements.

Permissions Used

  • Location — To calculate prayer times based on your city.
  • Notifications — To send Azan (prayer call) alerts at prayer times.
  • Boot on Startup — To reschedule alarms after device restarts.
  • Battery Optimization — To ensure Azan is never missed in background.
🔒

Data Security

  • All prayer time calculations happen entirely on your device. No data is sent to our servers.
  • We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers or any personally identifiable information.
  • The app does not require account registration or login.
