Privacy Policy

PrayerClock — Prayer Times & Azan App

Last updated: April 2025
📍

Location Data

  • Your device location is used only to calculate accurate prayer times for your city.
  • Location data is never stored on any server or shared with third parties.
  • You can switch to Manual Location in Settings at any time.
📊

Analytics & Performance

We use the following third-party services to improve app stability and user experience:

  • Firebase Analytics — Collects anonymous usage stats to understand how the app is used.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — Collects technical crash reports (e.g., device model, OS version) to fix bugs.
  • Firebase Realtime Database — Used only to store anonymous app configuration settings and update checks.

Essential Permissions

To provide core functionality, the app requires:

  • Overlay (Display over apps) — Used to automatically show the Azan screen when the phone is locked.
  • Battery Optimization Bypass — Ensures the system does not kill the app so Azan is never missed.
  • Notifications — To deliver Azan sounds and prayer time alerts.
  • Run at Startup — To reschedule prayer alarms after you restart your device.
🔒

User Data Security

  • We do not collect personal data like your Name, Email, or Phone Number.
  • All prayer time calculations happen locally on your device.
  • Anonymous device IDs (Android ID) are used only to manage app updates and remote configuration.
